About me

Theperson

I'm Rakshika — a marketing strategist and brand thinker currently completing my MSc in Management and Marketing at Leeds Beckett University.

My academic work spans corporate strategy, brand management, social media marketing, and AI's role in consumer behaviour — the subject of my dissertation, which examines how AI influences the purchasing journey in UK fashion retail using UTAUT2 and a mixed methods approach.

Before Leeds

I worked in B2B marketing in Switzerland at Greenteg, where I developed my grounding in campaign strategy, client communication, and working across European markets. That experience shaped how I think about the gap between brand intention and consumer reality.

What I observe

I pay close attention to retail environments, packaging design, and social media behaviour — and I write about what I notice on this blog. Marketing is everywhere if you know how to look.
